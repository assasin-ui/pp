About

Parkinson

Parkinson's Disease Detection focuses on developing a reliable and accurate method for early diagnosis of Parkinson's Disease using advanced machine learning and deep learning algorithms. By analyzing various biomarkers, including voice patterns, motor function data, and other clinical features, the system aims to enhance the precision of diagnosis, potentially enabling timely interventions and improved patient outcomes. This approach leverages data-driven techniques to identify subtle signs of the disease, supporting healthcare professionals in making more informed decisions.
